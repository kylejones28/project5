The Avatar FAQs

Who was the first ever Avatar??

The first ever avatar was named Wan, he learned to coexist with the spirits and was determined to help bring balance between them and mandkind.

What are the four elements in the Avatar??

What is the main message of Avatar The Last Airbender?

The main message of the show was how we should honor and respect others even though we have our differences. Equality and justice for all people no matter what culture or nation you come from.

What is the Order of the White Lotus?

The Order of the White Lotus is an ancient secret society that goes beyond the boundaries of the four nations. the Order of The White Lotus has a network of members spanning from kings to master benders.

Why is Azula the only Firebender who can create blue flames?

No one really knows why her bending is a blue flame but some of the dragons had differet colors besides the normal fire color. The colors spanned from yellow to purple but not trace of blue.
